Birthdays

In my family, July is a very busy month. Not only does my birthday fall in July, but also my brothers and sister. We spent our birthdays at home and invited a couple of people to our house for dinner. Me and my older brother both share a birthday on July 29, my younger sister's birthday is on July 26, and my little brother's is on July 16. At the end of the day, I got four books: Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (Sort Of), Land Of Stories, and Quidditch Through the Ages/Tales of Beedle Bard a few gift cards, and a red backpack.

The Cove

On my aunt's birthday, on June 30, we went to The Cove an Aquatic Center at Sierra Vista. There they had an indoor wave pool, two tube slides, diving boards, and a hot tub. Not only did I have a great time, but also my siblings and my parents.

Stay at Home

I spent most of my summer at home. Where I did chores, cooked, watched my siblings, TV and lots of other things. I also started watching new TV shows on Netflix such as Naruto, Fairytail, and Sword Art Online. During the summer, I also had piano lessons every week. I managed to create a Legend Of Zelda Medley arrangement by ear and also learned a few songs such as Toccata and Fugue in D minor and Stairway To Heaven.
